What is entropia?

For now, entropia is an idea, a project that brings cybersecurity to everybody. Because we all deserve to be protected on the Internet, we offer people an opportunity to become aware of how risky the digital world can be.

Far from only being about cybersecurity, entropia is made to bring people together and let people express their art or what they are good in. That’s why several entities of entropia would exist. Entropia is a singularity where all types of talent work together.
