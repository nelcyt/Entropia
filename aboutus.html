About us

Who are we ?

Hi I'm Nel !

I'm in my first year of networks, telecommunications and cybersecurity studying. My aim with this website is to give shape to the business I want to create while carrying out a project required of me for my studies. As mentioned in the other pages, Entropia's aim is to teach everyone about cybersecurity through field intrusion attempts (and more traditional means too) and debriefing/awareness-raising about dangerous actions in companies. The aim of this venture is to raise awareness of the dangers of cyberspace, and create a global digital consciousness.

I've always had a passion for computers, space and art. Entropia also aims to bring all these passions together under one roof. Because I think they may fit together very well to build something for passionate people like me. So anyone with talent but no one to guide them and give them a clear sense of purpose can work together to create a more beautiful and secure cyberspace.

Entropia is really a long-term project that will guide me through several stages of my life. In fact, I'd like to go for an engineering degree and gain experience in cybersecurity via aerospace, so entropia is a long way from being set up. That's why I see this project as a source of motivation and a goal to achieve in order to fulfill my ambitions. A project in which I can grow along with it.
