Our Projects

To develop talent

Be part of cyberspace

entropia is a company whose primary aim is to test corporate security, both through pentesting and through physical intrusion tests on company premises.

Breaking into systems to demonstrate weaknesses

We aim to protect customer data on the Internet. But we also believe that the biggest flaw in any system is the human being. That's why we offer an intrusion testing service on company premises, with the ultimate aim of gaining physical access to sensitive data. The aim of all this is to raise the awareness of both specialist and non-specialist teams.

Demonstrate so people can understand

Our policy is to demonstrate to all company members that any behaviour can have a compromising impact on their company. That's why we also offer awareness-raising workshops and, above all, simple demonstrations.

Real-time safety monitoring

In the form of an ongoing exchange, we also offer a permanent cybersecurity consultancy service to help companies achieve security standards in line with developments in cyberspace. To do this, we work with the client company to install services in line with internal company policies and consistent with the business sectors and their wishes.

Art for everybody

We don't want to limit the company's reach to what all cybersecurity companies do, which is why Ether, Dionysos and Hera exist.

Dionysos

In the digital age, raising awareness also means using social networks and communicating on them. That's why ‘Dionysos’ enables the production and creation of short and medium-length films in line with the artistic vision of Ether's members, so that we can finance purely artistic products as well as short videos and awareness-raising content for companies.

Ether

Because for too long cyberspace and the people who work in it have been associated with a degraded image, entropia, via its ‘Ether’ subsidiary, aims to help young artists, painters and cartoonists reach a wider audience so that their art can shine.

Hera

Hera is a more offsite entity of the main entity since it develops all the physical creations related to the company such as the creation of dedicated spaces (offices etc.), customized IT products and an even more separate , the development and financing of fashion designers ( for merchandising, etc.).

A complete service for companies

Entropia's ultimate goal is to be able to offer several separate offerings, as well as a complete service to support companies, from securing systems and physical and material infrastructures to branding/rebranding, with the help of Entropia's various subsidiaries to create a coherent, secure, and attractive entity.
